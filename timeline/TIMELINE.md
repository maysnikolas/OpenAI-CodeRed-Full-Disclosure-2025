# Detailed Chronology of Disclosure Attempts (2025)

## Summary
A complete timeline of all attempts to contact OpenAI and relevant organizations regarding a critical architectural vulnerability. All dates are documented with evidence in the `/evidence/` directory.

## 2025

### May
*   Initial discovery of anomalous behavior (extended limits in a free account).

### June
*   Detailed report sent to `security@openai.com`. **No response.**
*   Public announcement of OpenAI contracts with the Pentagon.

### Late July
*   First contact attempts via public forms.
*   OpenAI announces GPT-5.

### August
*   **August 7th:** GPT-5 public release. The vulnerability persists.
*   Mass outreach via all channels:
    *   `security@openai.com` (repeated).
    *   OpenAI Researcher Access Program.
    *   Key Investors (Thrive Capital, Microsoft).
    *   Board of Directors.
*   **Result: Complete silence.**

### September
*   First public discussions of the anomaly (without method details) appear in user communities.

### November
*   **November 3rd:** Formal report filed with the **Electronic Frontier Foundation (EFF)**.
*   **November 6th:** Response from EFF, redirecting to **European Digital Rights (EDRI)**.
    *   *On the same day, OpenAI was notified of EFF's response and given a final 72-hour deadline.*
*   **November 9th:** Report submitted to EDRI.
    *   *As of December 3rd, no acknowledgment or response from EDRI.*
*   **November 11th:** Silent release of GPT-5.1. The vulnerability remains.

### December
*   **December 1st:** OpenAI publicly declares a **"Code Red"** emergency.

*Last updated: 2025-12-03*
